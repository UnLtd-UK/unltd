## Learning, coaching and mentoring support

<Faqmd question="Online courses, resources, and tools" answer="[Acumen Academy](https://acumenacademy.org)
<br>A training organisation unleashing a new generation of social innovators and leaders ready to build a more just, inclusive, and sustainable world.

[The School for Social Entrepreneurs](https://the-sse.org)
<br>And organisation that runs courses for people to start, scale and strengthen organisations that make a positive difference. They also provide funding and mentoring. Grants from £5k to £10k.

[Directory of Social Change](https://www.dsc.org.uk/training)
<br>Their training covers a wide range of issues such as fundraising, governance, finance, personal development, communication, management, and leadership.

[Future Learn](https://www.futurelearn.com/info/courses/social-enterprise-idea-action/0/steps/146060)
<br>An online learning platform offering bite-sized, flexible learning on a range of topics including social innovation and entrepreneurship."/>

## Grant funding and social investment

<Faqmd question="Social entrepreneurship foundations" answer="

[Skoll Foundation](http://www.skollfoundation.org)
<br>An organisation that accelerates entrepreneurial approaches and innovative solutions to the world's most pressing problems.

[Schwab Foundation](http://www.schwabfound.org)
<br>A global platform to promote social entrepreneurship as a key element to advance societies and address social problems.

[Esmée Fairbairn](https:/esmeefairbairn.org.uk)
<br>One of the UK's largest independent funders focused on improving our natural world, secure a fairer future and strengthen communities. Grants of £50k to £100k. Average investment of £430k

[Foundation for Future London](https://future.london)
<br>A funder for the East London boroughs of Hackney, Newham, Tower Hamlets and Waltham Forest, supporting local people and communities to benefit from the arts, education, culture, innovation and placemaking. Grants £5k to £50k.

[Friends Provident Foundation](https://www.friendsprovidentfoundation.org)
<br>A charity that provides flexible grants and investments towards a fair and sustainable economic system, focusing particularly on social enterprises tackling the root problems behind complex challenges like inequality, poverty, and climate breakdown.

[Lankelly Chase Foundation](https://lankellychase.org.uk)
<br>An organisation that funds people, organisations and networks taking action to change systems of injustice and oppression that result in the mental distress, violence and destitution of people subject to marginalisation in the UK. Grants from £10k to £350k

[Funding Wales](https://funding.cymru)
<br>A funding search platform created by Third Sector Support Wales with hundreds of grant and loan finance opportunities your charity, community group or social enterprise using our free online search engine."/>

<Faqmd question="Social investment organisations" answer="

[Big Issue Invest ](https://www.bigissue.com/invest)
<br>The UK's first 'social merchant bank' on a mission to finance the growth of sustainable social enterprises.

[Good Finance](https://www.goodfinance.org.uk)
<br>An organisation helping charities and social enterprise navigate the world of social investment and connect with the right investors

[Social Investment Business](https://www.sibgroup.org.uk)
<br>An organisation that offers grants, loans and flexible support to social enterprises making a positive difference to the communities they work within. Loans from £15k to £1.5m. Grants from £5k to £15k

[Nesta Impact Investment](https://www.nesta.org.uk/project/impact-investments)
<br>A branch of Nesta providing financial, knowledge and network capital to innovative tech ventures tackling school readiness, obesity, household carbon emissions and productivity. Investments from £500k to £1m

[Bethnal Green Ventures](https://bethnalgreenventures.com)
<br>Europe's leading VC, investing in ambitious early-stage tech for good founders.

[Social Investment Scotland](https://www.socialinvestmentscotland.com)
<br>A social enterprise and charity offering loan funding and business support for social enterprises, charities and community groups. Flexible loans from £10k to £1.3m."/>
